## Rules from real trade data (27 markets, 13W/14L)

### 1. Hold to settlement, never sell early
- Sold early: 36% WR, avg -$0.37/trade
- Held to settlement: 100% WR, avg +$5.09/trade
- Every early exit lost money. Every hold-to-settlement won.

### 2. Only buy UP, disable DOWN
- UP trades: 57% WR (12W/9L)
- DOWN trades: 17% WR (1W/5L)
- DOWN signals are unreliable. Disabled entirely.

### 3. Entry at 60-120 seconds is optimal
- 60-120s: 56% WR (best)
- 120-180s: 14% WR (worst — counter-intuitive but confirmed by data)
